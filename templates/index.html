How to Play

  1. Host creates a game - Enter your name and click "Create Game"
  2. Share the QR code - Guests scan it to join instantly
  3. Guests get bingo cards - Each player gets a unique card with birthday-themed words
  4. Host calls out words - Players mark matching squares on their cards
  5. First to get BINGO wins! - Complete a row, column, or diagonal to win
